Information:

If you pass the mouse over a point, you'll be able to see more information on the councilor.
If you click on a point, you will select a councilor and you will be able to follow him in the different cluster.
If you drag-and-drop a point, it will stay at his place.
If you double-click, the points will move again to there cluster.
If you triple-click, the points will move a bit from their positions. It helps when a point is blocked.
